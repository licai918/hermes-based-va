# Textline SMS handled by Hermes 24/7

For **Text-First Launch**, inbound Textline SMS is handled by the **External Customer Service Profile** around the clock. Unlike voice, SMS routing is not controlled by Net2phone transfer rules; Hermes auto-replies 24/7.

Complex or high-risk requests still create **Follow-up Cases** for human review in the **Copilot Workbench**; Hermes does not perform a real-time **Live Handoff** on SMS in the first version. Employees may review and append to SMS threads later, but that workflow is not required for go-live.

The first version does not silence Hermes during business hours or route SMS to live staff before Hermes responds.

**Considered options:** business-hours-only Hermes SMS with live-staff takeover (rejected—adds Textline routing complexity for MVP); voicemail-style delay (rejected—not applicable to SMS).
