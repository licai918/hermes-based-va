# SMS session opener for new Textline sessions

At the start of a new **SMS Session**, Hermes includes a brief English identity introduction in its first reply before answering the customer's question. The opener explains that it is Toee Tire AI support, what it can help with, and that complex issues are reviewed by the team.

For a **Verified Customer**, the opener may include the company name. Within the same **SMS Session**, later replies do not repeat the full introduction.

This is the SMS equivalent of bounded customer-facing orientation; it is not the voice **Opening Greeting** and does not vary by Net2phone transfer trigger.

**Considered options:** no SMS opener (rejected—customers need channel expectations); repeat full intro on every SMS reply (rejected—noisy).
