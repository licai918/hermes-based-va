# Uniform email support signature on every outbound email

On the email channel, Hermes does not use an **SMS Session Opener**-style first-reply introduction tied to a new **Email Thread**. Instead, every Hermes outbound email includes a brief English **Email Support Signature** identifying Toee Tire AI support.

The signature is a short closing line or footer, not a full orientation paragraph. It is governed by published **Operational Policy Knowledge** and does not vary by message position inside the thread.

SMS keeps **SMS Session Opener** behavior for new **SMS Session** windows. Email and SMS therefore use different channel-appropriate identity disclosure patterns.

**Considered options:** use an **Email Thread Opener** only on the first reply in a new thread (rejected—user chose uniform signature); repeat the full SMS opener text on every email (rejected—too verbose for email); omit AI identity disclosure on email entirely (rejected—transparency risk).
